# Mohamed El Sharkawy - Personal Portfolio

Welcome to my GitHub repository! This repository contains my personal projects, contributions, and various resources I've worked on related to machine learning, computer vision, medical imaging, and more.

## About Me
I am a Graduate Research Assistant and Assistant Lecturer in the Computer Science Department at Assiut University. My research focuses on detecting eye diseases, such as diabetic retinopathy, using 3D OCT images. I am passionate about applying deep learning techniques to medical image analysis and contributing to healthcare technologies.

## Projects
Here are some of the projects you can explore in this repository:
- **3D OCT Image Analysis**: Methods for segmenting and analyzing retinal layers.
- **AI Models**: Implementations of deep learning models for medical diagnoses.
- **Research Publications**: Papers and work related to my research in computer vision and medical imaging.
